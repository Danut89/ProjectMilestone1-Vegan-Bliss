Vegan Pancakes

Category :
Breakfast
Cook Time :
30 Minutes
Nutrition :
150 kcal
Servings :
2-3

    Ingredients

  1. 1 cup all-purpose flour
  2. 2 tablespoons sugar
  3. 2 teaspoons baking powder
  4. 1/8 teaspoon salt
  5. 1 cup almond milk
  6. 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  7. 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

    Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt.
  2. Add almond milk, vegetable oil, and vanilla extract. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the pan.
  4. Cook until bubbles form on the surface, then flip and cook until golden brown on both sides.

    Nutrition (per serving):

  1. Calories: 150
  2. Protein: 3g
  3. Carbohydrates: 22g
  4. Fat: 5g
